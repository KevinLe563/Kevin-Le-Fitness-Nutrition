MEET KEVIN

Kevin is an aspirational young adult with passion in both the technical and fitness world. Since starting weight training in the summer of 2018, Kevin has grown his passion for both fitness and nutrition. He is working on balacing his focus on weightlifting and maintaining a healthy diet with his current studies at the University of Waterloo.
WORKOUT AND FITNESS

Over the course of his lifting career, Kevin has tried a full-body routine 3 times a week as well as a 2-day split. Currently, Kevin is running the 2-day split comprising of both upper and lower body designed towards both strength and muscle hypertrophy. Kevin's first day consists of working the chest, shoulders, as well as deadlifts, calves and abs. Kevin's second day consists of squats, hamstrings, back and arms. Cardio is integrated on off-days and through sports.
